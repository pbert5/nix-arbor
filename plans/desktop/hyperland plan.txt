
IMPLEMENTED: added desktop/hyprland and swapped desktoptoodle over to it.

- Hyprland uses the NixOS module and UWSM.
- XDG Desktop Portal Hyprland is supplied by the NixOS module, with the GTK
  portal retained as its file-picker fallback.
- PipeWire and WirePlumber are enabled.
- Hyprpolkitagent, SwayNC, Hypridle, Kitty, Rofi, Waybar, Awww, Hyprlock, and
  Dolphin are configured declaratively through the dendrite and its shared
  Home Manager module.
- Awww runs without imposing a wallpaper; selecting and managing wallpaper
  content remains planned.
